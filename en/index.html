Welcome to Inclusive Digital Academy

IDA App was created thanks to the support of the European Union and its Erasmus+ program. It is the result of an international collaboration between 11 partners, all committed to reducing the digital divide, especially for people with intellectual difficulties. We hope that you will find in it the necessary tools for a better understanding of the digital world and its daily use.

IDA is a training portal that will enable normal and mentally disabled individuals to safely use online tools (such as basic phone use, social media use, sending e-mails).
